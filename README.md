# AI_Early_Warning_Points_Checklist Lite v1.1

## Overview

The **AI_Early_Warning_Points_Checklist Lite** is a lightweight initial screening tool for identifying early governance warning points before or during AI adoption.

It is designed to help teams, consultants, and governance reviewers notice issues that may require follow-up review, clarification, or escalation before AI use becomes embedded in operational workflows.

This checklist focuses on practical early-warning points such as:

* use-case drift
* decision involvement
* data flow
* responsibility lines
* evidence and logging gaps
* nominal human oversight
* stop, escalation, and recovery conditions
* unmanaged or informal AI use

## Download

The Excel checklist is available in this repository:

* `AI_Early_Warning_Points_Checklist_v1.1.xlsx`

## Purpose

This tool is intended for early-stage AI governance screening.

It helps users ask:

* What AI use case is being considered?
* Is the AI system influencing decisions or actions?
* Are responsibility lines clear?
* Is there enough evidence to reconstruct what happened?
* Is human oversight meaningful?
* Are stop, escalation, and follow-up conditions defined?
* Are unknown answers treated as follow-up signals?

## How to Use

1. Download and open the Excel checklist.
2. Go to the **Early Warning Points** sheet.
3. Answer each item using:

   * Yes
   * No
   * Unknown
   * Not applicable
4. Review the **Summary** sheet.
5. Use the **Assessment Guide** to interpret Red / Yellow / Green outcomes.
6. Treat **Unknown** answers as follow-up signals, not as neutral or low-risk answers.

## Important Note

This checklist is not intended to replace formal legal, compliance, security, privacy, procurement, or risk-management review.

It is an initial screening tool. If significant risks, unclear responsibility lines, missing evidence, weak human oversight, or multiple Unknown answers are identified, further review should be conducted by appropriate internal or external specialists.

## Disclaimer

This material is provided for informational and practical screening purposes only.

It does not constitute legal advice, compliance certification, audit assurance, security assessment, or a complete AI risk assessment. Users should adapt the checklist to their organizational context, sector, jurisdiction, internal policies, and applicable regulatory requirements.
